# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** January 28, 2025

## Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how ZappaVault ("we", "our", or "us") handles information when you use our website located at https://zappavault.pages.dev (the "Service").

## Data Collection

**We do not collect personal information.** This site does not:
- Use cookies or tracking technologies
- Collect analytics or user behavior data
- Store personal information on our servers
- Require user registration or accounts

## Third-Party Services

The Service uses the following third-party services:

- **Cloudflare Pages:** Our hosting provider. Cloudflare may collect certain technical information (IP addresses, request logs) as part of their service. See [Cloudflare's Privacy Policy](https://www.cloudflare.com/privacypolicy/).

- **Dropbox:** File storage provider. Dropbox handles file storage and delivery. See [Dropbox's Privacy Policy](https://www.dropbox.com/privacy).

## Data Storage

No user data is stored on our servers. All content is served from:
- Cloudflare's CDN (content delivery network)
- Dropbox storage

## Logs

Cloudflare may log standard web server information (IP addresses, request URLs, timestamps) as part of their service. We do not actively collect or store these logs.

## Children's Privacy

Our Service is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
